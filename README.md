# Rentify: Smart Rental Platform

A multi-role rental platform that ensures transparency, safety verification, and smart pricing.

## 🚀 The Problem Statement

The traditional rental market is often plagued by a lack of transparency and safety assurance. Key challenges include:
- **Safety Concerns**: Renters have no reliable way to verify if a property meets safety standards before moving in.
- **Pricing Ambiguity**: Both owners and renters struggle with determining fair market value for rentals.
- **Fragmented Communication**: Coordinating between owners, renters, and maintenance staff is often chaotic and decentralized.
- **Manual Processes**: Payment tracking, reminders, and property management are frequently handled manually, leading to errors and delays.

## 💡 Our Solution: Rentify

Rentify transforms the rental experience by creating a safe, transparent, and efficient ecosystem for everyone involved.

### 1. Safety First
Through a dedicated **Inspector** role, every property can undergo a safety audit. Renters can view detailed inspection reports, providing peace of mind and ensuring high living standards.

### 2. Smart AI-Powered Pricing
We leverage **AI-based rent prediction** to provide data-driven pricing insights. This helps owners optimize their ROI while ensuring renters pay a fair, market-aligned rate.

### 3. Integrated Service Ecosystem
Beyond just listings, Rentify connects users with verified **Service Providers** for maintenance and repairs, keeping properties in top condition.

### 4. Seamless Interactions
- **Direct Communication**: Integrated WhatsApp chat for quick coordination.
- **Secure Payments**: Hassle-free rent payments via Razorpay.
- **Automated Reminders**: Never miss a payment or an inspection with our automatic notification system.
- **Visual Discovery**: Find the perfect home easily with Google Maps integration.

---

## 👥 User Roles

- **Property Owners**: Easily add and manage listings, track payments, and request safety inspections.
- **Renters**: Search for verified homes, pay rent securely, and access property safety reports.
- **Inspectors**: Conduct professional safety audits and provide digital verification for listings.
- **Service Providers**: Manage repair requests and offer services directly to property owners.
- **Admin**: Ensure platform integrity and manage the overall system.
